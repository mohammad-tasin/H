🔵 How to Play: Simon Says

A memory game with four colored buttons — 🔴 Red, 🟢 Green, 🔵 Blue, 🟡 Yellow. Watch the pattern, then repeat it!

Steps

  1. Click ▶ Start Round.
  2. Watch the buttons flash in a specific sequence.
  3. When the sequence stops, click the same buttons in the exact same order.
  4. If you're correct, press Start Round again — the sequence grows by one more step.
  5. One wrong click and the round resets. How long can you keep your streak?

Scoring

Each successful round adds 1 to your current score. The Best counter tracks your longest correct sequence across all rounds.

💡 Pro tip: Say the colour names out loud as they flash — turning visual information into words helps lock them into short-term memory.
