🔤 How to Play: Hangman

A hidden word is chosen at random and shown as blank dashes. Guess letters one at a time to reveal it before you run out of lives!

Steps

  1. Type a single letter (a–z) in the input box.
  2. Click Guess (or press Enter).
  3. If the letter is in the word, it fills in at all matching positions.
  4. If the letter is not in the word, you lose one ❤️ life.
  5. Reveal the full word before losing all 6 lives to win!
  6. Click New Word to start over with a different word.

Lives

You start with 6 ❤️ lives. Each wrong guess removes one. When all 6 are gone 🖤, the game ends and the word is revealed.

💡 Pro tip: Start with the most common English letters: E, T, A, O, I, N, S, R. Together they cover the majority of letters in most English words.
