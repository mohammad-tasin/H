💣 How to Play: Minesweeper Lite

An 8×8 grid hides 10 mines. Your goal is to open every safe cell without clicking on a mine.

Steps

  1. Click any cell to reveal it.
  2. A number shows how many mines are in the 8 surrounding cells.
  3. A blank cell means there are zero adjacent mines — it auto-expands.
  4. Clicking a mine ends the game immediately.
  5. Open all 54 safe cells to win!

Number Colours

Each number is colour-coded: 1 blue, 2 green, 3 red, 4 purple, and so on — use them to reason about where the mines are.

💡 Pro tip: Start by clicking a corner or edge cell — these reveal fewer neighbours but are statistically safer for your first click.
