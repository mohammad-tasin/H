🔴 How to Play: Connect Four

A 6-row × 7-column vertical grid. You drop 🔴 red discs; the CPU drops 🟡 yellow discs. First to connect four in a line wins!

Steps

  1. Click the ↓ button above any column to drop your red disc.
  2. Discs fall to the lowest empty row in that column.
  3. The CPU immediately plays after you.
  4. The first player to connect 4 discs in a row, column, or diagonal wins.
  5. If the grid fills up with no winner, it's a draw.

Winning Lines

  • Horizontal — 4 in the same row
  • Vertical — 4 in the same column
  • Diagonal — 4 diagonally (↗ or ↘)
💡 Pro tip: Control the center columns — more winning lines pass through the middle three columns than the edges.
