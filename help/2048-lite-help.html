🔢 How to Play: 2048 Lite

A 4×4 grid filled with numbered tiles. Slide them all in one direction — tiles of equal value merge and double. Reach the 2048 tile to win!

Controls

  • ← Arrow Left — slide all tiles left
  • → Arrow Right — slide all tiles right
  • ↑ Arrow Up — slide all tiles up
  • ↓ Arrow Down — slide all tiles down

Rules

  1. After every valid move a new tile (2 or 4) appears in a random empty cell.
  2. Two tiles of the same value that collide in a slide merge into one tile of double the value.
  3. Each merge adds to your score.
  4. The game ends when no more moves are possible.
💡 Pro tip: Keep your highest tile in a corner and never move away from that corner — this keeps large tiles adjacent and makes merging easier.
