🔢 How to Play: Guess the Number

The game picks a random number between 1 and 100. Your job is to find it in as few guesses as possible using the hot/cold hints.

Steps

  1. Type any number from 1 to 100 in the input box.
  2. Click Guess (or press Enter).
  3. Read the hint — Too low or Too high.
  4. Keep narrowing it down until you guess correctly!
  5. Click New Game to start a fresh round.

Scoring

The game counts your tries each round and tracks your best (lowest) try count across all rounds.

💡 Pro tip: Use binary search — always guess the midpoint of the remaining range. This guarantees you find the number in at most 7 guesses!
