⭕ How to Play: Tic-Tac-Toe

The classic 3×3 grid game. You play as X and the computer plays as O.

Steps

  1. Click any empty square on the 3×3 board to place your X.
  2. The computer automatically places its O.
  3. Continue taking turns until someone wins or the board fills up.
  4. Click New Game to reset the board at any time.

Winning

Get 3 of your marks in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — before the computer does. The score tracker records wins, losses, and draws across rounds.

💡 Pro tip: Always take the center square first if it's available — it gives you the most winning combinations (4 lines through the center vs 2 for edges).
