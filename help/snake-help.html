🐍 How to Play: Snake

Guide the snake around a 20×20 grid, eating food to grow longer. Don't crash!

Controls

  • ↑ Arrow Up — move up
  • ↓ Arrow Down — move down
  • ← Arrow Left — move left
  • → Arrow Right — move right

Rules

  1. The snake moves continuously in the current direction.
  2. Eating 🍎 food makes the snake one cell longer and increases your score.
  3. Hitting a wall ends the game.
  4. Running into your own tail ends the game.
  5. You cannot reverse direction (e.g. if going right, you can't go left).
💡 Pro tip: As the snake grows, hug the walls in a spiral path — this keeps the centre clear and prevents you from boxing yourself in.
