🃏 How to Play: Memory Match

A 4×3 grid of cards is shuffled face-down. Each emoji appears exactly twice. Find all 6 matching pairs!

Steps

  1. Click any hidden card to flip it and reveal the emoji.
  2. Click a second card to try to find its pair.
  3. If they match — both cards stay face-up and turn green.
  4. If they don't match — both flip back after a short pause.
  5. Remember where you saw each emoji to make faster matches.
  6. Match all 6 pairs to win!

Scoring

The game tracks Pairs found and total Moves made. Finish with fewer moves for a better score!

💡 Pro tip: When a card doesn't match, take a mental note of both its position and symbol — you'll need that info later!
