✂️ How to Play: Rock Paper Scissors

The timeless hand-game, now against a computer that picks at random. Best used to settle disputes — or just have fun!

How to Play

  1. Click the 🪨 Rock, 📄 Paper, or ✂️ Scissors button.
  2. The computer simultaneously picks one at random.
  3. The winner is determined by the classic rules below.
  4. Scores update automatically — play as many rounds as you like!

Rules

  • 🪨 Rock crushes ✂️ Scissors
  • ✂️ Scissors cuts 📄 Paper
  • 📄 Paper covers 🪨 Rock
  • Same choice = 🤝 Draw
💡 Fun fact: The computer picks completely at random, so no pattern-based strategy can beat it in the long run — it's pure luck!
